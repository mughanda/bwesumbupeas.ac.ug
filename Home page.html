KANO COFFEE FARMERS' ASSOCIATION

Kano coffee farmers' association was established in 1998 when kabale district had the production of coffee which was boosted by the government of Uganda.

During that time, Many people from Kabale engaged in coffee which impresssed the buyers to participate in coffee business. This also encouraged the propriators of the association to mobilize members to start an association that market their coffee and its products.
